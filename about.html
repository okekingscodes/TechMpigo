Mission

Our mission at TechMpigo is to create a supportive, inclusive, and collaborative environment that fosters learning, innovation, and positive social impact within the realm of technology. We are dedicated to helping our members grow, solve pressing societal issues through technology, and contribute to the advancement of our nation.

Vision

We envision TechMpigo becoming one of the leading tech communities in Africa, with over 5,000 members across nations within the next two years. In pursuit of our vision, we aim to establish physical locations with dedicated team members, allowing us to partner and collaborate with NGOs, companies, and government parastatals. Through these partnerships, we aspire to secure sponsorships to train tech enthusiasts, create job opportunities for our trainees.
